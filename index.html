About

Why Start Boxing?

Boxing is a sport that develops more than just your physique and punching ability. Whether in the ring, training like a boxer, or for self-defense, the sport of boxing instills many beautiful life-long skills that can benefit you both physically and mentally.

A Boxer's dermination.

You don't need to have aspirations of becoming the next Iron Mike to join a boxing gym. At Portland City Boxing we welcome all age groups, both men and women, young and old alike. With our personalized training, we will cater to your need and help you achieve your fitness goals through our staff of highly qualified boxers.

Learn more →

Build Strength

Boxing is a great sport to build strength throughout the entire body. Not only will you gain tone and muscle through repetition of punches, but the dynamic nature of movement and training that boxing emphasizes helps work other essential areas such as the core and legs.

Release Stress

Boxing serves as the ultimate outlet to punch back against stress. Studies have shown that rigorous exercise for 150 minutes weekly can drastically improve mental and physical health, and the demands of boxing can multiply these benefits by pushing you hard in the gym and demanding excellence in exercise.

Discover Discipline

A boxer's work ethic never sleeps, and an hour spent exercising the boxing way can often exceed tradional workouts by a a factor of 2x. With a mixture of conditioning, strength training, and practical boxing technique you will gain the discipline needed to succeed in and out of the ring.

A healthier life
